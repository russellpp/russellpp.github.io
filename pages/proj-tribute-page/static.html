LAURYN HILL

Lauryn Noelle Hill

Lauryn Noelle Hill (born September 4, 1981) is an American rapper, singer, songwriter, and actress. She is often regarded as one of the greatest rappers of all time. Hill is credited for breaking barriers for female rappers, popularizing melodic rapping and for bringing hip hop and neo soul to popular music. She is known for being a member of Fugees and her album The Miseducation of Lauryn Hill (1998), which became one of the best-selling albums of all-time.
